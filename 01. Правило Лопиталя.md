## Теорема 1
Пусть  
  
$$ \Large
f \in C([a,b]), \quad g \in C([a,b])  
$$  
$$ \Large 
g(x) \neq 0 \quad \text{при } \quad x \neq a  
$$  
$$  \Large
f(a) = g(a) = 0 \quad \exists f'(a), \quad g'(a) \neq 0
$$  
  
Тогда  
  
$$  \Large
\lim_{x \to a+0} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} =  
\frac{f'(a)}{g'(a)}  
$$  
  

  
### Доказательство  

$$  \Large
\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} =  
\frac{f(x)-f(a)}{g(x)-g(a)}=

\frac{\frac{f(x)-f(a)}{x-a}}  {\frac{g(x)-g(a)}{x-a}} \xrightarrow[x \to a+0]{}  \frac{f'(a)}{g'(a)}
$$

## Теорема 2

Пусть 

$$ \Large
f \in C([a;b]) \quad g \in C([a;b]) \quad f(a)=g(a)=0
$$

$$ \Large
g(x) \neq 0 \quad \text{при} \quad x \neq a
$$

$$ \Large
\forall x \in (a;b)  \quad \exists f'(x), g'(x) \neq 0
$$

$$ \Large
\exists \lim_{x \to a+0} \frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)} = A \in \overline{\mathbb{R}}
$$

Тогда

$$ \Large
\exists \lim_{x \to a+0} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = A 
$$


### Доказательство
По теореме Коши:

$$ \Large
\exists c = c(x) \in (a;x)
$$

$$ \Large
\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \frac{f(x)-f(a)}{g(x)-g(a)} = \frac{f(c(x))}{g(c(x))}
$$

$$ \Large
c(x) \xrightarrow[x \to a+0]{} a \Rightarrow \frac{f'(c(x))}{g'(c(x))} \xrightarrow[x \to a+0]{} A
$$
$$ \Large
\lim_{x \to a+0} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \lim_{x \to a+0} \frac{f'(c(x))}{g'(c(x))} = A
$$

## Теорема 3

Пусть
$$ \Large
f \in C([c;+\infty)), \quad g \in C([c;+\infty)), \quad c>0
$$
$$ \Large
\forall x \in [c;+\infty) \quad g(x) \neq 0
$$
$$ \Large
f(x) \xrightarrow[x \to + \infty]{} 0, \quad g(x) \xrightarrow[x \to + \infty]{} 0 
$$
$$ \Large
\forall x \in (c;+\infty) \quad \exists f'(x), g'(x) \neq 0
$$
$$ \Large
\exists \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)} = A \in \overline{\mathbb{R}}
$$

### Доказательство
Определим функции:
$$ \Large
F(0)=G(0)=0
$$
$$ \Large
\text{при } t \in (0; \frac{1}{c}]: \quad F(t) = f(\frac{1}{t}) \quad G(t) = g(\frac{1}{t}) 
$$
$$ \Large 
\Rightarrow \quad F(t) \xrightarrow[t \to +0]{} 0, \quad G(t) \xrightarrow[t \to +0]{}0 \quad \Rightarrow
$$
$$ \Large
\Rightarrow \quad F,G \in C([0; \frac{1}{c}])
$$
$$ \Large
\text{при } t \neq 0 \quad G(t) \neq 0
$$
$$ \Large
\forall t \in (0; \frac{1}{c}): \quad \exists F'(t) = f'(\frac{1}{t})*(\frac{1}{t})' = - \frac{1}{t^2}* f'(\frac{1}{t})
$$
$$ \Large
\quad \quad \quad \quad \quad \quad \quad \exists G'(t) = g'(\frac{1}{t})*(\frac{1}{t})' = - \frac{1}{t^2}* g'(\frac{1}{t})
$$
$$ \Large
\forall t \in (0; \frac{1}{c}) \quad G'(t) \neq 0
$$
$$ \Large
\frac{F'(t)}{G'(t)} = \frac{f'(\frac{1}{t})}{g'(\frac{1}{t})} \xrightarrow[t \to +0]{}A \text{(по условию)} \Rightarrow
$$
$$ \Large
\Rightarrow \text{по Теореме 2:} \quad \exists \lim_{t \to +0} \frac{F(t)}{G(t)}=A
$$
$$ \Large
\lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \lim_{x \to +0} \frac{F(x)}{G(x)} \text{ (по определению)}
$$
$$ \Large
\frac{f(\frac{1}{t})}{g(\frac{1}{t})} \xrightarrow[t \to +0]{} A
$$

## Теорема 4
Пусть
$$ \Large	
f \in C((a;b)),\quad g \in C((a;b))  
$$
$$ \Large
n \ge 2 \quad \forall x \in (a;b) \quad \exists f^{(n-1)}(x), \quad  \exists g^{(n-1)}(x)
$$
$$ \Large
\text{для} \quad x_{0} \in (a;b) \quad \exists f^{(n)}(x_0), \quad \exists g^{(n)}(x_0)
$$
$$ \Large
\text{при} \quad x \neq x_0 \quad g(x) \neq 0
$$
$$ \Large
f(x_0) = g(x_0) = f'(x_0) = g'(x_0) = ... = f^{(n-1)} = g^{(n-1)} = 0
$$
$$ \Large
\text{Но } \quad g^{(n)}(x_0) \neq 0
$$
Тогда
$$ \Large
\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} \xrightarrow[x \to x_0]{} \frac{f^{(n)}(x_0)}{g^{(n)}(x_0)}
$$
###  Доказательство
По формуле Тейлора с остатком в форме Пеано(1.78):
$$ \large
f(x)=f(x_0)+f'(x_0)(x-x_0)+...+\frac{1}{(n-1)!}f^{(n-1)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^{n-1} + \frac{1}{n!}f^{(n)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^{n} + \alpha (x)
$$
$$ \Large
\text{где}\quad \frac{\alpha (x)}{(x-x_0)^n} \xrightarrow[x \to x_0]{} 0
$$
$$ \large
g(x)=g(x_0)+g'(x_0)(x-x_0)+...+\frac{1}{(n-1)!}g^{(n-1)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^{n-1} + \frac{1}{n!}g^{(n)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^{n} + \beta (x)
$$
$$ \Large
\text{где}\quad \frac{\beta (x)}{(x-x_0)^n} \xrightarrow[x \to x_0]{} 0
$$
Теперь учтём условия:
$$ \Large
f(x)=\frac{1}{n!}f^{(n)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^{n} + \alpha (x)
$$
$$ \Large
g(x)=\frac{1}{n!}g^{(n)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^{n} + \beta (x)
$$
$$
$$
$$ \Large
\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \frac{\frac{1}{n!}f^{(n)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^{n} + \alpha (x)}{\frac{1}{n!}g^{(n)}(x_0)(x-x_0)^{n} + \beta (x)} = \frac{f^{(n)}(x_0) + n! \frac{\alpha (x)}{(x-x_0)^n}}{g^{(n)}(x_0) + n! \frac{\beta (x)}{(x-x_0)^n}} \xrightarrow[x \to x_0]{} \frac{f^{(n)}(x_0)}{g^{(n)}(x_0)}
$$
