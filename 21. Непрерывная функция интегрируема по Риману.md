## Теорема 

$$ \Large
f \in C([a,b]) \;\Rightarrow\; f \in R([a,b])
$$

### Доказательство
$$ \Large
\text{По т. Кантора(1.50): } f \in C([a,b]) \Rightarrow
$$

$$ \Large
\forall \varepsilon > 0 \;\exists \delta > 0 \text{ т.ч. }
\forall x_1,x_2 \in [a,b]:
$$

$$ \Large
|x_2 - x_1| < \delta \;\Rightarrow\; |f(x_2) - f(x_1)| < \varepsilon
$$
$$
$$
Пусть
$$ \Large
\exists P: \delta(P) < \delta, \quad P=\{x_k\}_{k=0}^{n}
$$

$$ \Large
\text{Рассмотрим } \ [x_{k-1},x_k]: \; f \in C([x_{k-1},x_k]) \Rightarrow
$$

$$ \Large
\Rightarrow \text{ по 2-ой т. Вейерштрасса(1.57) } \exists y_k^+, y_k^- \in [x_{k-1},x_k] \text{ т.ч. }
$$

$$ \Large
\forall x \in [x_{k-1},x_k]: \;
f(y_k^-) \le f(x) \le f(y_k^+)
$$

$$ \Large
M_k = f(y_k^+), \quad m_k = f(y_k^-)
$$

$$ \Large
\omega_k = M_k - m_k = f(y_k^+) - f(y_k^-)
$$

$$ \Large
|y_k^+ - y_k^-| \le x_k - x_{k-1} \le \delta(P) < \delta
$$

$$ \Large
\Rightarrow \omega_k = f(y_k^+) - f(y_k^-) < \varepsilon 
$$

$$ \Large
\sum_{k=1}^{n} \omega_k (x_k - x_{k-1})
<
\sum_{k=1}^{n} \varepsilon (x_k - x_{k-1})
=
\varepsilon (x_n - x_0)
=
\varepsilon (b-a)
$$

$$ \Large
\Rightarrow f \in R([a,b])
$$
