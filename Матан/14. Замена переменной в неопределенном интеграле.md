Пусть  
$$ \Large  
f \in C(a;b), \quad \omega \in C(p;q), \quad \omega'(t)\in C(p;q)  
$$  
  
$$ \Large  
\omega(t)\in(a;b) \quad \text{при } t\in(p;q)  
$$  
  
Тогда  
  
$$ \Large  
\int f(\omega(t))\omega'(t)\,dt = \int f(x)\,dx  
$$  
### Доказательство
$$ \Large  
F'(x)=f(x) \Rightarrow (F(\omega(t)))' = F'(\omega(t))\cdot \omega'(t) = f(\omega(t))\omega'(t)  
$$  
  
$$ \Large  
\int f(\omega(t))\omega'(t)\,dt = F(\omega(t)) + C  
$$
### Пример  
  
$$ \Large  
\omega(t)=at+b,\quad a\ne 0,\quad \omega'(t)=a  
$$  
  
$$ \Large  
\int f(at+b)\cdot a\,dt = \int f(x)\,dx  
\qquad x=at+b  
$$  
  
$$ \Large  
a\int f(at+b)\,dt = \int f(x)\,dx  
$$  
  
$$ \Large  
\int f(at+b)\,dt = \frac{1}{a}\int f(x)\,dx  
$$
