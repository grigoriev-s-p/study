## Теорема
$$
\Large
\text{Пусть } \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} u_n(x)\ \ \ u_n(x)\in C([a,b])
$$

$$
\Large
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} u_n(x) \underset{\substack{n \to \infty \\ x\in [a,b]}}{\rightrightarrows} S(x)
$$

$$
\Large
\Rightarrow\ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \int_a^b u_n(x)\,dx = \int_a^b S(x)\,dx
$$
## Доказательство
$$
\Large
S_n(x)=u_1(x)+\dots+u_n(x)\in C([a,b])
$$

$$
\Large
\int_a^b S_n(x)\,dx = \int_a^b u_1(x)\,dx + \dots + \int_a^b u_n(x)\,dx
$$

$$
\Large
 S_n(x)\underset{\substack{n \to \infty \\ x\in [a,b]}}{\rightrightarrows} S(x)\ \ (23)
$$

$$
\Large
\Rightarrow \int_a^b S_n(x)\,dx \xrightarrow[n\to\infty]{} \int_a^b S(x)\,dx \Rightarrow
$$

$$
\Large
\Rightarrow \int_a^b u_1(x)\,dx + \dots + \int_a^b u_n(x)\,dx \xrightarrow[n\to\infty]{} \int_a^b S(x)\,dx \Rightarrow\ 
$$

$$
\Large
\Rightarrow \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \int_a^b u_n(x)\,dx = \int_a^b S(x)\,dx
$$
