## Теорема

$$ \Large
f:[a,b], \; f \text{ монотонна } \;\Rightarrow\; f \in R([a,b])
$$
### Доказательство
$$ \Large
\text{Пусть } f \uparrow \;\Rightarrow\;
\forall x \in [x_{k-1},x_k]: \;
f(x_{k-1}) \le f(x) \le f(x_k)
$$

$$ \Large
\text{Пусть } \delta = \varepsilon \quad \text{и}\quad\exists P \text{ т.ч. } \delta(P) < \varepsilon
\;\Rightarrow\;
x_k - x_{k-1} \le \delta(P) < \varepsilon
$$

$$ \Large
 \Rightarrow
\omega_k = M_k - m_k = f(x_k) - f(x_{k-1})
$$

$$ \Large
\Rightarrow
\sum_{k=1}^{n} \omega_k (x_k - x_{k-1})
\le
\sum_{k=1}^{n} \omega_k \varepsilon
=
\varepsilon \sum_{k=1}^{n} \omega_k=
$$

$$ \Large
=
\varepsilon \sum_{k=1}^{n} (f(x_k) - f(x_{k-1}))
=
\varepsilon (f(b) - f(a))
$$

$$ \Large
\Rightarrow f \in R([a,b])
