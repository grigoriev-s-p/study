## Теорема(без док-ва)


$$
\Large
\text{Пусть } \{f_n(x)\}_{n=1}^{\infty}\quad  f_n:[a,b]\to\mathbb{R},\quad \forall n,\ \forall x\in[a,b]: \ \exists f_n'(x)
$$

$$
\Large
f_n' \in C([a,b])\ \forall n\ \quad \text{и} \ \quad \ f_n'(x)\underset{\substack{n \to \infty \\ x\in [a,b]}}{\rightrightarrows} \varphi(x)
$$

$$
\Large
x_0 \in [a,b] \quad \ \exists \lim_{n\to\infty} f_n(x_0)\in\mathbb{R}
$$

$$
\Large
\Rightarrow\ \exists f(x)\in C([a,b])\ \text{т.ч.}\ f_n(x)\underset{\substack{n \to \infty \\ x\in [a,b]}}{\rightrightarrows} f(x)\
$$

$$
\Large
\forall x\in[a,b]\quad \exists f'(x)\ \quad \ f'(x)=\varphi(x)
$$
