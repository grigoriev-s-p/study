## Теорема

$$
\Large
f \in C([a,b]), \quad
\omega \in C([p,q]), \quad
\omega' \in C([p,q])
$$

$$
\Large
\omega(t) \in [a,b], \quad t \in [p,q]
$$

$$
\Large
\omega(p)=a, \quad \omega(q)=b
$$

$$
\Large
\int\limits_{a}^{b} f(x)\,dx
=
\int\limits_{p}^{q} f(\omega(t))\,\omega'(t)\,dt
$$
